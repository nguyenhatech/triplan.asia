Muong Hoa Valley

Beautiful rice paddies dotted with farmhouses surrounded by mountain ranges. Visit Shin Chai village and Ta Phin village and experience the local lives in the most authentic way.

Can Cau Market

This very corlorful market runs every saturday. All locals are here to trade, buy, sale, eat, and drink. You can buy fabrics, artifacts, utensils and food.Try drinking corn wine with the locals!
