Thing to do


Muong Hoa Valley

Beautiful rice paddies dotted with farmhouses surrounded by mountain ranges. Visit Shin Chai village and Ta Phin village and experience the local lives in the most authentic way.

Can Cau Market

This very corlorful market runs every saturday. All locals are here to trade, buy, sale, eat, and drink. You can buy fabrics, artifacts, utensils and food.Try drinking corn wine with the locals!

Ham Rong Mountain

Do this half day trek , for this amazing view point - get the overview of Sapa, see the beautiful flower garden, heaven’s Gate and cloud yard.

Fansipan

Trek to the peak of Fansipan Mountain, one of highest mountains in Southeast Asia. Fansipan offers some of the finest trekking trails that can be found on the Indochina Peninsula. Gaze at the breathtaking views from the path to, and from, the summit.
